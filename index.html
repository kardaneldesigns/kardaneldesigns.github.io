Hello

I'm Karissa Enns

I Help People Turn Their Visual Business Dreams Into Reality

As a bright young graphic designer fresh out of College, I have all kinds of time and ideas to help you get your dream up and running! But first, let me tell you a bit about who I am before you choose to hire me.

Firstly , and most importantly, I absolutely love Jesus. It's not a cringy religious belief, but rather a love-centered lifestyle. This means that Love, Compassion, and Loyalty are the foundations of who I am as a person, and I believe those are some of the greatest qualities people look for in someone they are trusting with their money.

I was born and raised in the suburban city of St. Catharines, Ontario, but I am a country girl at heart. Currently I live in the small town of Jordan, Ontario, where I get to enjoy the fresh air and peacefulness a small town offers. And as cliche as it sounds, I have always been an art person. It slowly grew on me the older I got, before one of my family members told me about the profession of Graphic Design. I will be honest, at first I wasn't so sure it was for me, but I decided to try it anyways. Well, here I am. A college graduate from Niagara College's Graphic Design program. Brimming with excitement to share what I have learned with you!

I've had my share of experiences already. The typical minimum wage jobs, and a few side design ones in the mix. All which have taught me valuable lessons about the honesty and loyalty that people like you look for in a person. And that's what I work towards every day. As someone who has seen a world of dishonest and "in it for the money" professionals, I want to be anything but that. I am a designer for no other reason than because I genuinely love it. It gets me excited in the morning, and keeps me up at night with new ideas.

I am excited to work with you to make your design dreams become reality!
